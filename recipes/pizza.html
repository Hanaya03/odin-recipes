How to Make Pizza

A soft, baked dough topped with an aged dairy and tomato-based sausce.

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Cheese(any kind)
  • Marinara sauce
  • pepperoni(optional)

Steps:

  1. preheat oven to 500 degrees farenheit for 30 minutes
  2. Flatten the dough into a circle
  3. spread teh marinara sauce over the dough
  4. Spread the cheese over the sauce
  5. Add the pepperoni(optional)
  6. place the pizza into the oven
