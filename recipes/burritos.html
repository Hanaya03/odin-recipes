How to Make Breakfast Burritos

A bland breakfast for when you want to feel full.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Cheese(any kind)
  • Onion
  • Green Pepper
  • Bacon
  • Flour Tortilla

Steps:

  1. Begin by dicing the onion, and chopping the green pepper into thin strips, and scrambling the eggs.
  2. Place the bacon into a pan with oil.
  3. Toss the diced onion into another pan until it's caramelized.
  4. In that same pan, place the green pepper.
  5. Put the scrambled egg with cheese into another pan.
  6. Cook the flour tortilla
  7. once everything is cooked, place it all neatly into the tortilla, wrap it up, and enjoy.
