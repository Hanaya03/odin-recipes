How to Make Brownies

A tasty dessert, best enjoyed with milk.

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Salt
  • Vanilla
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps:

  1. Melt the butter with the sugar, salt, and cocoa powder
  2. Add the vanilla, eggs, and chocolate chips to the mix
  3. Add the flour
  4. Pour the batter into a pan and place it into the oven for 20 minutes
  5. Once they appear ready, pull them out to cool and enjoy.
